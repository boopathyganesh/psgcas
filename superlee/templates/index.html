About Us

Career Development Center

The Career Development Centre of PSG College of Arts & Science works towards helping the students to explore their professional interests and map them to different career choices like higher education, competitive exams, entrepreneurship, and campus placements.

It is involved in developing the competency levels of the students in order to match their skills with those required by the industry.
